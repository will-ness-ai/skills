Add the `grilling-frontend-prototyping` skill — converge on a frontend look through rounds of prototypes and grilling verdicts.

Each round builds 5 radically different variants of the current design question into one standalone mocked app (a draggable picker restyles it live, with toggles for meaningful states), and the grilling walks the visual design tree from overall look down to component groups and individual components. Registered in the plugin manifest and both READMEs, with Codex metadata alongside.
